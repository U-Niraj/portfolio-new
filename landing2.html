Computer Aided Designing

CAD uses software for precise 2D/3D modeling, optimizing engineering and architecture design.
It enhances efficiency by enabling detailed design analysis and optimization.
CAD empowers designers to create intricate, optimized representations, revolutionizing design processes.

Objectives

The objectives of learning CAD at FabLab include mastering the fundamentals of computer-aided design software, developing proficiency in 2D and 3D modeling techniques, and gaining practical skills for engineering and design projects. Additionally, the course aims to foster creativity, problem-solving abilities, and a deep understanding of industry-standard CAD practices.

Day 1

On day 1 at FabLab, we explored Fusion 360, diving into 2D shapes like circles and polygons. We learned tools for drawing, editing, and dimensioning, setting the stage for 3D modeling. Excited by this intro, we're ready for deeper dives into creative design and advanced techniques in Fusion 360!

Day 2

On day 2 at FabLab, we ventured further into Fusion 360, focusing on 3D drawings and essential commands like extrude. We honed our skills in creating three-dimensional shapes from scratch and learned how to use extrude and related commands to add depth and complexity to our designs. This hands-on experience elevated our understanding and enthusiasm for 3D modeling!

  • Day 2 assignment

Day 3

On day 3 at FabLab, we delved into the fascinating process of creating sketches from 3D models in Fusion 360. This involved learning techniques to extract 2D sketches from our 3D designs, enabling us to further refine and detail our models with precision. It was a pivotal step in mastering Fusion 360's capabilities for both 3D modeling and 2D drafting!

  • day 3 assignment

Day 4

On day 4 at FabLab, we delved into the world of assembly design, learning about moving parts and mechanisms in Fusion 360. We explored techniques for creating assemblies with dynamic components, understanding how parts interact and move within a design. With dedicated time to create our own designs, we unleashed our creativity and applied our newfound knowledge to bring our ideas to life!

  • Day 4 assignment

Day 5

On day 5 at FabLab, we had the exciting opportunity to work on a complex design of our choice in Fusion 360. With dedicated time and support from mentors, we tackled challenges, refined our skills, and brought our unique ideas to fruition. The guidance from mentors helped us overcome doubts and create impressive designs, marking a significant milestone in our Fusion 360 journey!

  • Day 5 assignment

What we learned

By the end of the module at FabLab, we gained a comprehensive understanding of Fusion 360's capabilities. We mastered 2D sketching, 3D modeling, assembly design, and learned to create moving parts and complex designs. With mentor support, we tackled doubts and honed our skills, culminating in the ability to transform ideas into intricate and functional digital designs.
